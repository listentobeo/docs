## 1. Create your account

Go to [AIHDStudio](https://homedesignerstudio.vercel.app) and sign up with your email. New accounts start with **free credits** to try the tools immediately.

## 2. Take or find a photo of your room

<Tip>
Use a wide-angle shot from a corner of the room. Good lighting and a clutter-free space produce the best AI results. See the [Photo Guide](/tips/photo-guide) for details.
</Tip>

Your photo should:
- Be well-lit (natural light is best)
- Show the full room or the area you want redesigned
- Be at least 800px wide
- Be in JPG or PNG format

## 3. Choose a tool

From your **Dashboard**, pick the tool that matches your goal:

| Goal | Tool |
|------|------|
| Redesign the whole room | [AI Designer](/tools/ai-designer) |
| Change wall color | [Wall Color Changer](/tools/wall-color-changer) |
| See artwork on your wall | [Art Visualizer](/tools/art-visualizer) |
| Rearrange furniture | [Layout Ideas](/tools/layout-ideas) |
| Find specific products | [Product Finder](/tools/product-finder) |
| Create social content | [UGC Studio](/tools/ugc-studio) |

## 4. Upload and generate

1. Upload your room photo
2. Set your preferences (style, color, mood, etc.)
3. Hit **Generate**
4. Wait 10–30 seconds for the AI to process

## 5. Save and share

Once your design is ready:
- **Save to Gallery** — stored automatically in your account
- **Add to Mood Board** — organize designs by project or room
- **Share** — get a public link to share with friends, family, or your contractor
- **Download** — save the image to your device
