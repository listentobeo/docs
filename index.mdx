## What is AIHDStudio?

AIHDStudio is an AI-powered interior design platform that lets you redesign your home without lifting a paintbrush. Upload a photo of any room and instantly see it transformed — new furniture layouts, wall colors, art on walls, and full style makeovers — all generated by AI in seconds.
