## What are credits?

Credits are consumed each time you run an AI generation in AIHDStudio. Think of them as generation tokens — one generation = a set number of credits deducted from your balance.

Your credit balance is always visible in the top navigation bar.

## Credit costs by tool

| Tool | Credits per generation |
|------|----------------------|
| AI Room Designer | 2 credits |
| Art on Wall Visualizer | 2 credits |
| Layout Ideas | 2 credits |
| UGC Studio | 2 credits |
| Wall Color Changer | 1 credit |
| Product Finder | 1 credit |
| Design Planner | **Free** — no credits |

## When are credits deducted?

Credits are deducted **when a generation starts**, not when it completes. If a generation fails due to a technical error, your credits are refunded automatically.

## Free credits

New accounts receive a starter credit allowance to try the tools. The exact amount is shown when you sign up.

## Running out of credits

When your balance hits 0:
- A prompt appears to upgrade your plan or purchase more credits
- The Design Planner remains accessible — it never requires credits
- Any in-progress generations complete normally

## Credit balance

Your current balance is always shown in the navigation bar. You can also check it in **Settings → Your Plan**.
