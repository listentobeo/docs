## Monthly credits

Paid plan credits **refresh every month** on your billing date. Credits do not roll over — unused credits expire at the end of the billing period.

## Checking your plan

Go to **Settings → Your Plan** to see your current plan, credit balance, next billing date, and upgrade options.
