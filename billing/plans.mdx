## Monthly vs one-time credits

Paid plan credits **refresh every month** on your billing date. Credits do not roll over — unused credits expire at the end of the billing period.

## Checking your plan

Go to **Settings → Your Plan** to see:
- Your current plan name
- Current credit balance
- Next billing date
- Upgrade options
