## Overview

The Dashboard is your home base in AIHDStudio. From here you can:

- Launch any AI design tool
- View and manage your personal designs
- Track your credit balance
- Access the community Gallery and Mood Boards

## Tool Grid

The center of your Dashboard shows all available tools as cards. Each card displays:

- **Tool name and icon**
- **Credit cost** — how many credits one generation costs
- A **Pro** or **Business** badge for features locked to paid plans

Click any tool card to open it directly.

## Recent Designs

Below the tool grid, your **personal AI-generated designs** are displayed as a horizontal scroll — these are your private files, visible only to you.

Each card shows a **before/after comparison** so you can instantly see the transformation. Click any card to open the full **Project Detail** view with download, share, mood board, and Gallery publishing options.

<Info>
Recent Designs is your personal library. Designs only appear in the community **Gallery** if you explicitly choose to add them. See [Gallery](/designs/gallery) for details.
</Info>

## Credit Balance

Your current credit balance is shown in the top navigation bar. Credits are consumed each time you run a generation. See [How Credits Work](/billing/credits) for a full breakdown.

## Navigation

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop">
    The left sidebar contains links to:
    - **Dashboard** — home and your personal recent designs
    - **Gallery** — community design feed
    - **Mood Boards** — your design collections
    - **Settings** — account, appearance, plan
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile">
    The bottom navigation bar contains:
    - **Home** — Dashboard
    - **Gallery** — community feed
    - **Boards** — Mood Boards
    - **Settings** — account settings
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Design Planner (Free)

At the bottom of the Dashboard you'll find a card for the **Design Planner** — a free AI chat tool for planning renovations and design projects. No credits required, ever. [Learn more →](/tools/design-planner)
