## Overview

The Dashboard is your home base in AIHDStudio. From here you can:

- Launch any AI design tool
- View your recent designs
- Track your credit balance
- Access your Gallery and Mood Boards

## Tool Grid

The center of your Dashboard shows all available tools as cards. Each card displays:

- **Tool name and icon**
- **Credit cost** — how many credits one generation costs
- A **Premium** badge for tools available on paid plans

Click any tool card to open it directly.

## Recent Designs

Below the tool grid, your most recent AI-generated designs are displayed as a horizontal scroll. Each card shows a **before/after comparison** so you can instantly see the transformation.

Click any recent design to open the full **Project Detail** view with download, share, and mood board options.

## Credit Balance

Your current credit balance is shown in the top navigation bar. Credits are consumed each time you run a generation. See [How Credits Work](/billing/credits) for a full breakdown.

## Navigation

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop">
    The left sidebar contains links to:
    - **Dashboard** — home
    - **Gallery** — all your saved designs
    - **Mood Boards** — your design collections
    - **Settings** — account, appearance, preferences
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile">
    The bottom navigation bar contains:
    - **Home** — Dashboard
    - **Gallery** — your designs
    - **Boards** — Mood Boards
    - **Settings** — account settings
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Design Planner (Free)

At the bottom of the Dashboard you'll find a promotional card for the **Design Planner** — a free AI chat tool that helps you plan renovations and design projects without using any credits. [Learn more →](/tools/design-planner)
