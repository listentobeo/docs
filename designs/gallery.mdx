## What is the Gallery?

The Gallery is your permanent design library. Every time you generate a design with any AIHDStudio tool, it's automatically saved here. You never lose a design — even ones you didn't explicitly save.

## Navigating the Gallery

From the bottom nav (mobile) or left sidebar (desktop), tap **Gallery** to open it.

Your designs are displayed as cards showing:
- The generated output image
- The tool used to create it
- The date it was generated
- The style or color applied

## Filtering and searching

Use the filter bar at the top of the Gallery to narrow your view:
- **By tool** — show only AI Designer results, Wall Color results, etc.
- **By date** — most recent first (default) or oldest first
- **By style** — filter to a specific design style

## Opening a design

Click any design card to open the **Project Detail** view, which shows:
- A full **before/after comparison** (for designs generated after the before/after feature was added)
- The style, tool, and date
- Options to download, share, add to a Mood Board, or add notes

## Organizing with Mood Boards

From any design's detail view, click **Add to Mood Board** to collect it into a themed board. See [Mood Boards](/designs/mood-boards) for details.

## Deleting designs

To delete a design, open it and tap the delete option. Deleted designs cannot be recovered.

<Warning>
Deleted designs are permanently removed and cannot be restored.
</Warning>
