## What is the Gallery?

The Gallery is a **community space** — a public feed of designs that AIHDStudio users have chosen to share. It's a place to browse real room transformations for inspiration, discover what's possible with each tool, and publish your own best work.

<Info>
Your personal design files are not automatically in the Gallery. They live in the **Recent Designs** section on your Dashboard. The Gallery only contains designs you've explicitly chosen to share.
</Info>

## Browsing the Gallery

From the bottom nav (mobile) or left sidebar (desktop), tap **Gallery** to explore the community feed.

Designs are displayed as cards showing:
- The before/after transformation
- The tool and style used
- The creator's display name

Click any card to see the full design detail.

## Publishing your design to the Gallery

To share a design to the community Gallery:

1. Open a design from your **Recent Designs** (Dashboard) or from the **Project Detail** view
2. Click **Add to Gallery**
3. Your design is published to the community feed

<Note>
Only designs you explicitly add will appear in the Gallery. Your other designs remain private to your account.
</Note>

## Your personal designs

Your private collection of all AI-generated designs lives in **Recent Designs** on your Dashboard — not in the Gallery. From there you can:

- View all your past generations
- Open any design's full before/after comparison
- Download, share, or add to a Mood Board
- Choose to publish selected designs to the community Gallery

## Removing a design from the Gallery

If you want to remove a design you've published:
1. Open the design from your Dashboard
2. Click **Remove from Gallery**

The design remains in your personal Recent Designs — only the public Gallery listing is removed.
