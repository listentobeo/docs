## What are Mood Boards?

Mood Boards are collections of your AI-generated designs, organized however you like. Use them to:

- Group all designs for a specific room (e.g. "Master Bedroom Renovation")
- Compare different style directions for the same space
- Build a visual brief to share with an interior designer or contractor
- Save inspiration as you explore styles before committing

## Creating a Mood Board

1. Go to **Boards** in the navigation
2. Click **New Board**
3. Give your board a name (e.g. "Living Room Refresh 2026")
4. Start adding designs

## Adding designs to a Mood Board

From any design in your Gallery or a Project Detail view:
1. Click **Add to Mood Board**
2. Select an existing board or create a new one
3. The design is instantly added

## Viewing and sharing a Mood Board

Open a board to see all designs in a grid layout. From the board view you can:

- **Rearrange designs** — drag to reorder
- **Remove designs** — remove individual items from the board (they remain in your Gallery)
- **Share the board** — get a public link so others can view your collection without needing an account
