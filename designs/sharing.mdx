title: "Sharing Designs"
description: "Share any design or Mood Board with a public link. No account required to view."
---

## Sharing a single design

From any design's **Project Detail** view:

1. Click the **Share** button
2. A public link is generated (e.g. `aihdstudio.com/share/abc123`)
3. Copy the link and share it anywhere — WhatsApp, email, Instagram DM, etc.

The recipient can view the full before/after comparison without needing an AIHDStudio account.

## Sharing a Mood Board

From any Mood Board:

1. Click **Share Board**
2. Copy the public link
3. Share with anyone

Shared boards are view-only — visitors cannot edit or delete your designs.

## Privacy

- Shared links are **unlisted** — they won't appear in search engines unless you share them publicly
- You can **revoke** a shared link at any time from the design's detail view
- Revoking a link makes it immediately inaccessible to anyone who had it

## Use cases

- Send your AI design concepts to a partner or family member for feedback
- Share with an interior designer or contractor before a consultation
- Post the link on social media to show off your transformation
- Send to a paint supplier or furniture store to match products to your design
