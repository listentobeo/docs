## What it does

The AI Designer takes a photo of your existing room and regenerates it in a completely new interior design style. The AI:

- Replaces furniture with style-appropriate pieces
- Adjusts color schemes, textures, and materials
- Adds decor, lighting, and rugs
- Preserves your room's architecture (doors, windows, floor plan)

<Info>
The AI keeps structural elements like doors, windows, ceiling height, and floor unchanged. Only furniture, decor, and soft furnishings are transformed.
</Info>

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your room photo">
    Click **Upload Photo** and select a clear, well-lit photo of your room. See the [Photo Guide](/tips/photo-guide) for best results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a style">
    Select your desired interior style from the dropdown.

    **Starter plan styles:**
    - Minimalist
    - Modern
    - Scandinavian
    - Contemporary

    **Pro & Business styles (full library):**
    - Japandi
    - Industrial
    - Bohemian
    - Mid-Century Modern
    - Coastal
    - Traditional
    - Farmhouse
    - Art Deco
    - *(and more)*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add optional preferences">
    Optionally describe specific preferences in the text field — for example: *"keep it bright with light wood tones"* or *"I want lots of plants"*.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate Design**. The AI processes your photo in 15–30 seconds and delivers a fully redesigned room image.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the output

The result shows your room completely restyled. Key things to know:

- **Spatial accuracy**: The AI uses door frames (~2.1m) as a scale reference to size furniture correctly
- **80cm clearance**: Walking paths between furniture pieces are maintained automatically
- **Locked elements**: Doors (including color, handles, and finish), windows, floors, and ceilings are never altered

## Saving your design

After generation, your design is automatically saved to your **Recent Designs** on the Dashboard. You can also:

- **Add to Mood Board** — collect designs for a specific room or project
- **Share** — get a shareable link
- **Download** — save to your device
- **Generate a variation** — create a new version with slight changes (1 credit)

## Tips for best results

<Tip>
Show the full room in your photo. Partial shots (just a corner or just a sofa) give the AI less context and produce less accurate results.
</Tip>

- Use natural light or bright indoor lighting
- Avoid cluttered or dark photos
- For large rooms, stand at the corner to capture the most space
- The AI works best on living rooms, bedrooms, dining rooms, and home offices

## Troubleshooting

**The AI changed my door color**
The latest version of the AI is instructed to lock door color, material, texture, handle, and finish identical to the original. If this still occurs, try regenerating.

**Furniture looks too large/small**
The AI uses your door frame as a scale reference. Make sure the door is visible in your photo for accurate spatial reasoning.

**Result doesn't match the style I selected**
Add more detail in the optional preferences field — for example: *"strict minimalist, only black/white/grey palette, no clutter"*.
