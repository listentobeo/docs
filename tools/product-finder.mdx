## What it does

Product Finder uses AI to understand what you're looking for and surfaces product suggestions that match your description, style preference, and budget range. Instead of endlessly scrolling furniture sites, describe what you want and let the AI do the searching.

## Free vs paid plan access

<Warning>
Free plan users get **1 test search** with Product Finder. After that, continued access requires a Pro or Premium plan. Your free search is a full-featured trial — use it to explore what the tool does before upgrading.
</Warning>

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe what you're looking for">
    Be as specific or as general as you like. Examples:
    - *"A mid-century modern coffee table in walnut, under $400"*
    - *"Minimalist floor lamp for a home office"*
    - *"Japandi-style dining chairs, set of 4"*
    - *"Something to put in the corner of my living room that adds warmth"*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set optional filters">
    Optionally set:
    - **Style** — to narrow to a specific design aesthetic
    - **Budget range** — to keep suggestions within your price range
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search">
    Click **Find Products**. The AI returns a curated list of product suggestions with descriptions of why each one fits your criteria.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips

<Tip>
Mentioning your room's existing style helps a lot. Try: *"my room is Scandinavian with light wood tones — looking for a statement rug"*.
</Tip>

- The more context you give, the better the suggestions
- You can follow up with refinements: *"show me something darker"* or *"smaller scale options"*
- Combine with the AI Designer — generate a room redesign, then use Product Finder to source the specific pieces shown
