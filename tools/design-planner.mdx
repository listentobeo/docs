## What it does

The Design Planner is a free AI chat tool that acts as your personal interior design consultant. Use it to:

- Plan a renovation or full room redesign before you start generating
- Get style recommendations based on your preferences and budget
- Create a detailed design brief that you can reference throughout your project
- Ask questions about furniture placement, color theory, and design principles

<Note>
The Design Planner is **completely free** — it does not use any credits. Use it as much as you want.
</Note>

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Design Planner">
    Click **Design Planner** from your Dashboard (labeled as a free tool).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your brief (optional)">
    The sidebar has a **Design Brief** form where you can specify:
    - Room type (living room, bedroom, kitchen, etc.)
    - Your preferred aesthetic style
    - Budget range
    - Any specific requirements or constraints

    Filling this in helps the AI give more targeted advice — but you can also just start chatting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Chat with the AI">
    Ask anything about your design project. Good starting points:
    - *"I have a small 10x12 bedroom — how should I approach it?"*
    - *"Help me build a Japandi-inspired living room on a $2,000 budget"*
    - *"What colors work well with dark wood floors?"*
    - *"I want to redesign my home office — what do I need to consider?"*
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the AI can help with

| Topic | Example questions |
|-------|------------------|
| Style selection | *"What's the difference between Japandi and Scandinavian?"* |
| Color theory | *"What accent colors go with a sage green sofa?"* |
| Furniture planning | *"How do I make a small living room feel bigger?"* |
| Budget planning | *"What should I prioritize buying first when redesigning a bedroom?"* |
| Material selection | *"Pros and cons of linen vs velvet for a sofa?"* |
| Renovation advice | *"Should I paint or wallpaper an accent wall?"* |

## Tips

<Tip>
Use the Design Planner before using the AI Designer. Build your brief here first, then use what you've planned to guide your style selection in the generation tools.
</Tip>

- Be specific about your constraints (budget, rental restrictions, kids/pets)
- Share photos of rooms you love — describe what you like about them
- The AI remembers your conversation history, so you can refer back to earlier suggestions
