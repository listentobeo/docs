## What it does

UGC Studio (User Generated Content Studio) helps you create polished before-and-after social media content from your AIHDStudio designs. It formats your transformations into shareable visuals optimized for Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, and other platforms.

## Credit cost

| Action | Credits |
|--------|---------|
| Generate UGC content | 2 credits |

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select your designs">
    Choose a before photo (your original room) and an after photo (your AI-generated design). You can use any design from your Gallery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a content format">
    Select from available formats:
    - **Side-by-side comparison** — before and after shown side by side
    - **Swipe reveal** — formatted for Instagram carousel or TikTok swipe
    - **Split screen** — dramatic half-and-half split
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add captions (optional)">
    Add a headline or caption to overlay on the content. Examples:
    - *"Before → After: Living Room Glow Up"*
    - *"What AI designed for my apartment 🏠"*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Create Content**. Your formatted content is ready to download and post.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices for social media

<Tip>
The most viral design content shows a dramatic contrast. The stronger the transformation between your before and after, the better the content performs.
</Tip>

- Choose designs with bold style changes for maximum visual impact
- Add an engaging caption that invites comments (*"Which do you prefer?"*)
- Use the split-screen format for TikTok and Reels — it performs best as a thumbnail
- Post your content with the hashtags: `#interiordesign #homedesign #AIdesign #roomtransformation`
