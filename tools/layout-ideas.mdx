## What it does

Layout Ideas analyzes your room photo and generates alternative furniture arrangement suggestions. Instead of redesigning the style, it focuses purely on **how furniture is positioned** — traffic flow, focal points, and functional zones.

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your room photo">
    Upload a photo of your current room layout. A wide-angle shot showing the full floor plan works best.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your lifestyle (optional)">
    Add notes about how you use the space. Examples:
    - *"We entertain often — need space for 6+ people"*
    - *"I work from home — need a desk area"*
    - *"Two young kids — needs open floor space for play"*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Get Layout Ideas**. The AI generates furniture arrangement suggestions shown as annotated design concepts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the AI considers

- **Traffic flow** — clear walking paths between entry points and key areas
- **Focal points** — orienting seating toward fireplaces, TVs, or windows
- **Conversation zones** — grouping seating so people face each other
- **Natural light** — positioning reading chairs and workspaces near windows
- **Scale** — using door height as a reference to ensure furniture fits proportionally

## Tips

<Tip>
Describe your room dimensions if you know them (e.g. "12ft x 16ft living room"). The AI uses this to suggest appropriately sized layouts.
</Tip>

- If you have specific pieces you want to keep, mention them: *"keep the sectional sofa, only rearrange the rest"*
- For open-plan spaces, specify which zone you want to focus on
