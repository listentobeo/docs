## What it does

The Art Visualizer composites your artwork into a photo of your room — realistically scaled, properly lit, and framed. You can choose from preset frame styles or upload a photo of your own custom frame.

## Credit cost

| Action | Credits |
|--------|---------|
| Visualize artwork | 2 credits |

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your room photo">
    Upload a photo of the wall where you want to hang the artwork. The wall should be clearly visible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your artwork">
    Upload the artwork image. This can be:
    - A photo of a painting or print you own
    - A digital art file
    - A photo you've taken that you want to display as art
    - Any PNG or JPG image
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a frame style">
    Select from the preset frame options:

    | Frame | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | **Modern** | Thin flat black metal frame with clean minimalist edges |
    | **Ornate Gold** | Baroque gold frame with carved floral and scroll details |
    | **Natural Wood** | Warm oak wood frame with visible grain and matte finish |
    | **Frameless** | Edge-to-edge display with no frame |

    Or upload a **custom frame photo** — the AI will replicate the exact style of any frame you photograph.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Visualize**. The AI places your artwork on the wall with correct perspective, shadow, and lighting in 15–25 seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Custom frames

You can upload a photo of any physical frame — from a vintage thrift store find to a specific frame you found online — and the AI will apply that exact style to your artwork visualization.

To use a custom frame:
1. Take a clear photo of the frame (close-up, good lighting)
2. Select **Custom Frame** in the frame selector
3. Upload your frame photo
4. Generate as normal

<Info>
The AI sends your custom frame image as a visual reference to match the style, color, and finish as closely as possible.
</Info>

## Tips for best results

<Tip>
Use a straight-on wall shot for the most accurate scale and perspective. Side angles work but may distort the artwork placement.
</Tip>

- The wall in your photo should be relatively blank where you want to place the art
- For best scaling, include some furniture or a door in your room photo so the AI can judge proportions
- High-resolution artwork images produce sharper final results
- Frameless works especially well for large-format photography prints
