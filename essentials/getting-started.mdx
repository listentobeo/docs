Welcome to **AIHDStudio**.

AIHDStudio helps you visualize:
- Wall colours
- Art on your walls
- Interior redesign ideas
- Photo-to-art transformations

## How to use AIHDStudio

1. Upload a clear photo
2. Select a tool
3. Adjust options (style, colour, mood)
4. Generate your result

## Best photo tips
- Use good lighting
- Avoid blurry images
- Show the full wall or room
- JPG or PNG works best

Use the **Help** button inside the app anytime.
